# Streamer

Retro Creator turns real game data — score, lives, bosses, records — into overlays and live automations. Full product documentation: [Retro Creator wiki](https://nelfe80.github.io/RetroCreator-Wiki/).

## Getting started

1. **Install Retro Creator** on your Windows streaming PC (free Lite edition to try) — it detects APIExpose (RetroBat) automatically, or runs in demo mode.
2. **Compose your overlay** in the designer: score, timer, now playing, achievement popup widgets… bound to live data.
3. **Add the overlay** to your streaming software as a browser source. Play: everything updates live.

## Live Contest: your viewers play at home (Studio edition)

1. **Create the contest**: game, objective (race, best score, time attack, survival) — the editor only offers signals that really exist in the game.
2. **Test round first**: every participant validates their signals before opening — the dashboard shows who is ready. Never skip this step before a public contest.
3. **Open**: viewers type your !command, the bot replies with a personal short link, their game launches at home and pauses on the starting line.
4. **Synchronized start**: in-game 5-4-3-2-1 countdown, live standings, CSV export + JSON feed to show the podium in your overlays.

## Venue tournaments on stream

If you also host tournaments in a venue equipped with the Fleet Hub, the venue's tournament screen is a web page — add it to your scene like any browser source. See the [organizer guide](organisateur.md).
