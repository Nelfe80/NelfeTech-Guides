# Cabinet builder

You build and sell RetroBat cabinets to individuals? Ship them with the APIExpose engine and its Data Pack preinstalled: dynamic marquees, lit panels, live scores across 14,000+ mapped games — ready to play out of the box.

## The journey

1. **Buy a Builder key** (per cabinet sold) on [nelfetech.com/builders](https://nelfetech.com/builders.html) — delivered by email.
2. **Preinstall** APIExpose + Data Pack on the cabinet before delivery.
3. **Keep the key** with the sales invoice: it is your proof. Nothing to activate on the customer side — their personal use stays free.

## Volume and limits

- **Volume discounts** via dedicated codes from 10 cabinets — contact us.
- **Commercial** operation of cabinets (venue, bar, paid events) falls under Fleet Hub licenses ([venue manager guide](gestionnaire.md)) — a nice upgrade path argument for your pro customers.
