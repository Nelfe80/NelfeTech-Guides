# Player online / at home

The NelfeTech account links your venue scores, your Live Contest participations and — down the road — rankings and rating.

## Create your account

On the platform's account area: enter your **email**, open the **login link** you receive (valid 30 minutes). No password to remember.

- **Twitch** can be linked to the account ("Continue with Twitch") — handy if you join Live Contests, never mandatory.
- **RetroAchievements** will be linkable as a verified link (preparing home rankings).

## Claim your venue scores

Your venue badge carries a **recovery code**: it is your claim code.

1. Log into your account.
2. Enter your badge's recovery code.
3. Your scores from that venue ("verified venue" provenance) join your profile — visit after visit, venue after venue.

!!! tip "Several venues?"
    Claim each badge: all your anonymous identifiers converge into the same account.

## Rankings

Public rankings show the best marks per player and per game, with their provenance. Venue scores are signed by the venue's hub — a third party can verify their origin. The **rating** (verified arcade and home, side by side) will arrive after its calibration season.

## At home

Streamers' Live Contests are played from home: see the [streamer guide](streamer.md) for the viewer journey (one !command in chat, the game launches at home, your runs count live).
